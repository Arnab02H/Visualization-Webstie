Analysis of Sentiment Impact on Gold Prices

Another critical part of the analysis was exploring how the sentiment of news articles influenced gold price movements. The Price Sentiment column captured whether news articles had a positive, negative, neutral, or no sentiment.

A pie chart was created to visualize the distribution of sentiments. The pie chart provides a clear representation of how many instances fall under each sentiment category, enabling a quick understanding of overall market sentiment regarding gold prices.

Key Insights:

A significant portion of news articles expressed negative sentiment, followed by positive sentiment. This could suggest that negative news is often associated with the observed downward trend in gold prices.
